Facilities


FOR  A  COMPLETE   VACATION

Enjoy state-of-the-art facilities meant to make your stay in Sri Lanka’s pristine South Coast enriched and memorable – there’s something for everyone here at one of the best hotels in Unawatuna – Araliya Beach Resort and Spa Unawatuna. Start you mornings off right with a reinvigorating yoga session by the beach, plunge into our infinity pool to escape the heat of the day and enjoy a refreshing cocktail at the Sky Deck to cap the day off. For the adventurous types, we also have exciting off-site excursions – from exciting water sports to our very own diving school!

Yoga & Meditation

Relieve yourself from all your worries, revive, and rejuvenate your mind, body and soul with Yoga in Unawatuna! Join our well-curated yoga and meditation sessions carried out by talented yoga instructors during your stay.

Swimming Pools

Take a break from the midday heat by diving into one of our lavish swimming pools – it’s the perfect way to refresh yourself and relax as the tropical sun reaches its zenith.

Fitness Center

Stay in sync with your fitness regime even when you are on vacation! Head out to our fully-equipped fitness centre for a wholesome and revitalising workout session.




Water Sports

The azure blue waters that border the sandy shore of Unawatuna are ripe for some exciting excursions and experiences – namely water sports!.There are plenty of thrilling adventures for you to choose.

Children's Play Area

Let your kids run wild and enjoy themselves at our kids’ play area, it’s the perfect way to set your kids free while you take a load off and relax, here at our exquisite Unawatuna Family Resort.

Signature Lounge

Located on the ground floor overlooking our exquisite beach club, the VIP Lounge is a modern and comfortable space that is dedicated to offering a tranquil space for our guests.
